# Complete Account Management and Security Guide

Maintaining the security and integrity of your account is our top priority. This document covers everything from password resets to advanced security features.

## 1. Logging In and Password Resets
### Standard Login
You can log in to the dashboard using your registered email and password. If your organization has Enterprise SSO enabled, you must log in using your Identity Provider (e.g., Okta, Google Workspace, Azure AD) by clicking the "Sign in with SSO" button.

### Forgot Password
If you forget your password:
1. Navigate to the login page and click "Forgot Password".
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. You will receive an email containing a secure reset link. This link expires after 15 minutes.
4. Click the link and enter your new password. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and contain a mix of uppercase letters, numbers, and special symbols.

*Note: If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, please check your Spam/Junk folders. If your corporate firewall blocks external automated emails, ask your IT admin to whitelist the `resolveai.com` domain.*

## 2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA / 2FA)
We strongly encourage all users to enable Multi-Factor Authentication to protect against credential stuffing and phishing attacks.

### How to Enable MFA
1. Log in to your account and navigate to **Settings > Security**.
2. Click the toggle to **Enable Authenticator App**.
3. A QR code will appear on your screen. Open your preferred authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator, or 1Password) and scan the QR code.
4. Enter the 6-digit Time-Based One-Time Password (TOTP) generated by your app to verify the setup.

### Backup Recovery Codes
Immediately after enabling MFA, you will be provided with 10 single-use recovery codes. **You must save these in a secure location.** If you lose your phone or accidentally delete your authenticator app, these codes are the *only* way to regain access to your account. Our support team cannot bypass MFA on your behalf.

## 3. Managing Email Addresses and Notifications
### Changing Your Primary Email
To change the email address associated with your account:
1. Go to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Enter your new email address in the email field and click "Save Changes".
3. For security reasons, we require a dual-confirmation process. An email will be sent to your *current* email address notifying you of the change, and a confirmation link will be sent to the *new* email address.
4. You must click the confirmation link in the new email inbox to finalize the change.

### Notification Preferences
You can manage what emails you receive from us under **Settings > Notifications**. 
- **Security Alerts:** Cannot be disabled. These include new login alerts from unknown IPs and password changes.
- **Billing Alerts:** Includes invoice receipts and failed payment warnings.
- **Marketing & Product Updates:** Can be freely toggled off at any time.

## 4. Data Privacy and Account Deletion
### GDPR and CCPA Compliance
You have the right to request a full export of all data associated with your account. To request a data takeout, navigate to **Settings > Privacy** and click "Export Data". A ZIP file containing your data in JSON format will be emailed to you within 72 hours.

### Permanent Account Deletion
If you wish to terminate your service and delete your data:
1. Go to **Settings > Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. You will be asked to type your password and the phrase "DELETE" to confirm.

**Warning:** This action is entirely irreversible. Once triggered, all active API keys are immediately revoked, your subscription is canceled, and your databases are dropped. Backups containing your data are purged from our cold storage within 30 days in strict accordance with GDPR data retention policies.
